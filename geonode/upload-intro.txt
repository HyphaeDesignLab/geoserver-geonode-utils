  -----------------------------------------------------------------
   Genode Layer/Data Auto-uploader
  -----------------------------------------------------------------

  You can upload a single/multiple files/file-sets by:
    passing upload director(ies) and/or file-path(s) as arguments          OR
    getting prompted at the command line for each file/directory to upload

    one or multiple file-path(s), director(ies), or BOTH are allowed
    file types: .shp, .geojson, .tif, .zip
